How to make simple tomato soup

Prep Time: 5 mins
Cook Time: 20 mins
Total Time: 25 mins
Servings: 6
Yield: 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes in juice
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 (10 ounce) can condensed tomato soup
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon margarine
  • ½ teaspoon chicken base
  • ¼ teaspoon ground white pepper
  • 2 cups whole milk

Directions

Step 1

Simmer diced tomatoes in juice in a saucepan over low
heat until hot, 7 to 10 minutes. Add baking soda and let
the mixture foam. Stir tomato soup, parsley, sugar,
margarine, chicken base, and white pepper into the
tomato mixture; cook until hot, about 5 minutes. Pour
milk into tomato mixture and stir; cook until hot, about 5
minutes.
